Core Features

Advanced AI Detection

Utilizes powerful ONNX and PyTorch models for fast and accurate enemy detection, compatible with both CPU and GPU (CUDA/DirectML) for maximum performance.

Human-Like Aiming

Features a sophisticated PID controller that ensures smooth, natural, and human-like mouse movements, avoiding jerky and obvious robotic aiming.

Deep Customization

Fully adjustable settings including FOV size, detection confidence, and aiming speed. Switch between simple and advanced modes to match your preference.

Precision Targeting

Choose your aim target—head or body. Fine-tune the hitbox ratios to ensure the AI targets exactly where you want it to.

Automation

Automate your actions with features like Auto-Fire and fully configurable keybindings for aiming, toggling, and firing.

Preset Manager

Save and load your favorite configurations with the built-in preset manager. Create, share, and import settings for different games or scenarios.
